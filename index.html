Disclosure about theme saving

Selecting Remember this choice means that anselmschueler.com will store the theme you select in your browser’s local storage, and will retrieve it when you open the site again later.

This data isn’t communicated to anybody and stays on your device.

If you’re using a modern browser, you can delete this data in the browser’s settings. It might also be called Cookies or Site Data or something similar.

When you select a theme, that theme is saved in your browser’s session storage. This is done so that the theme you selected stays selected when you reload the page or visit a different sub-page.

This data is immediately deleted when you leave the site.

I use Cloudflare’s analytics to analyze how many people visit this site, how, and from where. The data I get from this is not granular. I only see aggregated data, and can’t identify individual visitors. Data about location and other details is only shown in categories.

You are not being tracked while using the site.
Only visits to different sub-pages, the initial loading of the page, and refreshing the page, generate any data.
