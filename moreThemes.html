Disclosure about data storage for themes

  • Selecting a theme means that anselmschueler.com will store the theme in your browser's temporary session storage.

    The session storage is automatically deleted when you close the page.

  • Selecting Remember this choice means that anselmschueler.com will store the theme in your browser's local storage, and will retrieve it when you open the site or reload it.

    If you're using a modern browser, you can delete this site's local storage in the settings. It might be called Cookies or Site Data or something similar.

  • All of this data isn't communicated to anybody and stays on your device.
